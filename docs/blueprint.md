# **App Name**: Ivoria Systems Digital Showcase

## Core Features:

- Interactive Company Overview: Dynamic display of Ivoria Systems' mission, values, and expertise.
- Service Portfolio Visualization: Visually engaging presentation of services like AI-driven platforms and cloud infrastructure.
- Case Studies Explorer: Interactive case studies showcasing successful projects and client solutions.
- AI-Powered Content Recommendation: Recommend relevant services or case studies based on user interests using AI analysis of their behavior on the website.
- Client Testimonials Carousel: Rotating display of client testimonials to build trust and credibility.
- Contact Form with Intelligent Routing: A contact form that uses AI to determine the optimal department to send inquiries to based on keywords.
- Blog and News Section: Display latest insights and company updates.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep, sophisticated Indigo (#4B0082) to evoke intelligence and resilience.
- Background color: Light Lavender (#E6E6FA), a very desaturated tint of the primary, creating a calm and elegant backdrop.
- Accent color: Muted Violet (#8A2BE2) to highlight key sections and calls to action, contrasting effectively with the Indigo.
- Body text: 'Inter', a sans-serif with a neutral, modern look; for a clean reading experience.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', a proportional sans-serif giving a techy feel for impactful titles and headings, paired with 'Inter' for the body.
- Use clean, minimalist icons in the violet accent color to represent different services and features.
- A clean, modern layout with clear sections for company overview, services, case studies, and contact information. Utilize white space to create a sense of elegance.
- Subtle animations and transitions to enhance user experience and create a polished feel. For example, fade-in effects for content and smooth scrolling.
